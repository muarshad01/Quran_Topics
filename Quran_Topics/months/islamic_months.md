### Hijri calendar
* The 12 months of the Islamic calendar, also known as the Hijri calendar, are: 
1. Muharram,
2. Safar,
3. Rabi' al-Awwal,
4. Rabi' al-Thani,
5. Jumada al-Awwal,
6. Jumada al-Thani,
7. [Rajab (Allah SWT Month)](https://github.com/muarshad01/Quran_Topics/blob/main/Rajab_month.md),
8. [Sha'ban (My Month)](https://github.com/muarshad01/Quran_Topics/blob/main/Shaban_month.md),
9. Ramadan (Ummat Month),
10. Shawwal,
11. Dhu al-Qidah,
12. Dhu al-Hijjah

#### 4 Sacred Months: 1 (Muharram), 7 (Rajab), 11 (Dhu al-Qidah), 12 (Dhu al-Hijjah)
