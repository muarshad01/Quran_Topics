#### [The Major Sins of the Heart](https://www.ramadhan.org.uk/sins/sins-of-the-heart/)
* Heart has 10 impurities (Al-Ghazali [1058 – 1111])
* Unless our heart is not PURIFIED (Aina-e-Dil is not purified), God doesn't live there.

***

1. Arrogance (Kibr) -- 1
2. Ostentation / Showing Off (Riya) -- 2
3. Envy (Hasad) -- 3
4. Hatred (Bughd) -- 4
5. Miserliness (Bukhl) -- 5
6. Love of the world (Wahn) -- 6
7. Anger (Ghadab) -- 7
8. Excessive speech (Loquaciousness)
9. Greed (Tama)
10. Heedlessness (Ghaflah) -- 10
11. Rancor (Ghill)
12. Vanity (Ghurur).
13. Conceit (Ujb)
14. Deceit (Ghish)
15. Backbiting (Ghibah)

***

1. __Arrogance and conceit (Kibr and Ghurur)__: Considering oneself superior to others and being deluded by one's own perceived importance or deeds.
2. __Ostentation (Riya')__: Performing good deeds or showing positive traits not for God's sake, but to gain praise, fame, or social status from people.
3. __Jealousy and envy (Hasad)__: Disliking the blessings that God has bestowed upon others and wishing for their removal.
4. __Hatred and rancor (Hiqd and Ghill)__: Harboring deep-seated anger, aversion, and malice towards another person over time.
5. __Stinginess/miserliness (Bukhl)__: An unwillingness to spend wealth or give charity, even when one has the means.
6. __Love of the world/materialism (Hub al-dunya)__: Excessive attachment to wealth, position, and worldly possessions, often described as a root cause of other diseases.
7. __Anger (Ghadab)__: Uncontrolled rage that can lead to harmful words and actions and cloud one's judgment.
8. __Excessive speech (Loquaciousness)__: Engaging in useless, idle, or harmful talk (such as backbiting, lying, or arguing), which hardens the heart.
9. __Greed/covetousness (Tama')__: An intense and insatiable desire for more than what one needs or rightfully owns.
10. __Heedlessness (Ghaflah)__: Being forgetful of one's purpose in life, the afterlife, and one's relationship with the Creator.
