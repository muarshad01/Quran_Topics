#### Ulul-Azm (أُولُو ٱلْعَزْمِ) 
* Elite __Messengers of FIRM Resolve__ in the Quran, a title for the FIVE prophets known for extraordinary patience and major divine missions:
1. Noah (Nuh)
2. Abraham (Ibrahim)
3. Moses (Musa)
4. Jesus (Isa)
5. Muhammad (peace be upon them all), who brought new laws (Sharia) and faced immense hardship, serving as ultimate examples of prophetic endurance and steadfastness in faith, as mentioned in Quran.
