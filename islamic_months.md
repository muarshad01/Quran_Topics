### Hijri calendar
* The 12 months of the Islamic calendar, also known as the Hijri calendar, are: 
1. Muharram,
2. Safar,
3. Rabi' al-Awwal,
4. Rabi' al-Thani,
5. Jumada al-Awwal,
6. Jumada al-Thani,
7. Rajab,
8. Sha'ban,
9. Ramadan,
10. Shawwal,
11. Dhu al-Qidah,
12. Dhu al-Hijjah
