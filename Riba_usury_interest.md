* __Najash (shill bidding)__ is a prohibited form of bid manipulation in Islamic law, where a person falsely raises the price of an item without intent to buy, deceiving others. It is forbidden by the Sunnah, based on Hadith, and violates principles of honesty, fairness, and consent in trading, often involving collusion

* Quranic Verses Addressing __Market Manipulation__ and Dishonesty 
Surah Al-Mutaffifin (83:1-3): "Woe to those who give less [than due]..." - This verse directly condemns fraudulent trading practices, such as providing short measure or manipulating product quality.
Surah At-Tawbah (9:34): "...And those who hoard gold and silver and spend it not in the way of Allah – give them tidings of a painful punishment." This refers to hoarding, which is a form of artificial scarcity that drives up prices.
Surah Al-Baqarah (2:188): "And do not consume one another's wealth unjustly or send it [in bribery]..." This applies to all forms of taking money through deceit, such as manipulating a product's price or value
