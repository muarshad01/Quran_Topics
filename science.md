A "singularity" generally refers to a point where current models and equations break down. The concept appears across three major fields:
1. **Gravitational Singularity (Physics & Astronomy)** At the core of a black hole, matter is crushed into an infinitely dense, infinitely small point where space-time curves to infinity. **At this point, the laws of physics—including Einstein's Theory of General Relativity—break down.** It is widely believed that a theory of quantum gravity is needed to truly explain what happens at this point.
