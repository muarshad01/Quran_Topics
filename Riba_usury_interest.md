Key Quranic Verses Regarding Riba:
Surah Al-Baqarah (2:275-281): This is the most comprehensive passage, asserting that "Allah has permitted trade and forbidden riba". It warns that those who consume riba will stand on Judgment Day like those driven to madness by Satan.
Surah Al-Baqarah (2:278-279): These verses declare a "war from Allah and His Messenger" against those who persist in taking interest, demanding they stop and return to the principal amount only.
Surah Ali 'Imran (3:130-132): Prohibits consuming riba "doubled and multiplied," urging believers to fear Allah and avoid the fire prepared for disbelievers.
Surah An-Nisa (4:161): Mentions riba as an injustice for which previous nations were punished.
Surah Ar-Rum (30:39): States that riba does not increase in the sight of Allah, whereas zakat (charity) does
