#### What are the 5 books of the Torah?
* The Torah, or Five Books of Moses (Chumash), consists of 
1. Genesis
2. Exodus
3. Leviticus
4. Numbers
5. Deuteronomy.

* These books, which form the foundation of Jewish scripture, outline the creation of the world, the stories of the patriarchs, the Exodus from Egypt, and the receiving of the law. 

#### The Talmud (Tafsir)
* The is the central, comprehensive collection of Jewish law, ethics, customs, and traditions, serving as the foundation of religious life.

#### Bible
* The Bible is a collection of 66 (Protestant) to 73 (Catholic) sacred books divided into:
  * The Old Testament (before Jesus) and
  * The New Testament (after Jesus).
* It begins with Genesis, detailing creation, and ends with Revelation, detailing the end times.
