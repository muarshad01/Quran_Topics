* __Consmic Constant__: Einstein's cosmological constant `(Λ)` was a term he added to his general relativity equations in 1917 to create a stable, static universe, acting as a "fudge factor" against gravity's pull, but he later called it his "biggest blunder" after Hubble's discovery of an expanding universe. * __Dark Energy__: In 1998, astronomers discovered the universe's expansion is accelerating, not slowing down due to gravity, using distant Type Ia supernovae as "standard candles" to measure cosmic distances. __This led to the concept of dark energy, a mysterious force.__
