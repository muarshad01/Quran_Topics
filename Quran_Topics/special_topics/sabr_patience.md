Sidq-e-Khalil (AS) Bhi Hai Ishq, Sabr-e-Hussain(RA) Bhi Hai Ishq
Maarka'ay Wajood Mein Badar-o-Hunain Bhi Hai Ishq

The truthfulness of Abraham is but a form of Love, and so is the patience of Husain—
